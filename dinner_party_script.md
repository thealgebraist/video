# Movie Trailer Production Script: "Dinner Party Hell"
**Duration:** 30 Seconds
**Genre:** Comedy / Social Horror
**Voiceover Style:** Sophisticated yet increasingly unhinged narrator.
**Music Bed:** Elegant string quartet that becomes discordant and chaotic.
