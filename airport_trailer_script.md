# Movie Trailer Production Script: "Airport Hell"
**Duration:** 30 Seconds
**Genre:** Comedy / Horror
**Voiceover Style:** Gritty, dramatic movie trailer voice.
**Music Bed:** Intense, building orchestral tension that breaks into a comedic tuba/bassoon theme.

---

| Scene | Visual Description | Audio / VO |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **01** | **Wide shot:** A massive, never-ending security line snaking through a grey, sterile airport terminal. People look exhausted. | **VO:** "In a world... where time stands still." |
| **02** | **Close up:** A frustrated man trying to take off a very complicated boot while holding a plastic bin with his teeth. | **SFX:** Grunting, plastic bin rattling. |
| **03** | **Close up:** A sad, wilted $18 ham sandwich wrapped in crinkly plastic under a flickering heat lamp. | **VO:** "Where a sandwich costs more than your soul." |
| **04** | **Medium shot:** A gate agent with a cold stare pointing at a "DELAYED" sign that just flipped from 1 hour to 6 hours. | **SFX:** *Ding* of airport announcement. |
| **05** | **Action shot:** A crowd of "gate lice" hovering around the boarding area like zombies before their group is called. | **VO:** "The battle for Group 9 begins." |
| **06** | **Interior shot:** A passenger squeezed into a tiny middle seat between two massive bodybuilders. Their knees are hitting their chin. | **SFX:** Creaking airplane plastic. |
| **07** | **Close up:** A screaming infant with its mouth wide open, red-faced, sitting directly behind a passenger trying to sleep. | **SFX:** Ear-piercing baby scream. |
| **08** | **Medium shot:** The passenger in front abruptly reclining their seat, smashing the laptop of the person behind them. | **SFX:** *CRACK* of plastic. |
| **09** | **Close up:** A tiny plastic cup of tomato juice vibrating violently during extreme turbulence. | **VO:** "Buckle up..." |
| **10** | **Wide shot:** A lone, battered suitcase circling an empty baggage carousel in a dark, empty hall. | **VO:** "...because you're never leaving." |
| **11** | **Title Card:** AIRPORT HELL. Coming to a terminal near you. | **Music:** Final dramatic orchestral hit followed by a sad trombone. |
| **12** | **Post-Title:** A passenger realizes they left their passport in the seat pocket of the plane they just exited. | **VO:** "Oh no." |
