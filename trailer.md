# Movie Trailer Production Script: "A Dalek Comes Home"
**Duration:** 120 Seconds
**Genre:** Sci-Fi / Heartwarming Drama / Absurdist Comedy
**Voiceover Style:** Deep, gravelly "Don LaFontaine" style trailer voice.
**Music Bed:** Starts with dark industrial synth, transitions to warm acoustic/Americana guitar strings, builds to an orchestral emotional crescendo.

---

| Time | Visual Image Prompt | Sound Effects (SFX) & Music Layer | Voiceover (VO) Script |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0:00-0:15** | **Wide Shot:** A bleak, metallic alien landscape (Skaro). jagged towers of steel, grey skies, smoke. Thousands of Daleks moving in formation. <br><br> **Close Up:** A single Dalek (identifiable by a small scratch) staring blankly at a desolate robotic factory. | **Music:** Dark, brooding, metallic throbbing drones. <br> **SFX:** Marching mechanical sounds, distant laser fire, wind howling through metal. | **(Deep, Serious Voice)**<br>"In a universe... of infinite hate... where emotion is a crime... and compassion is deleted..." |
| **0:15-0:25** | **Close Up:** The Dalek's eye-stalk zooms in on a piece of rusted scrap metal that looks vaguely like a horseshoe. <br><br> **Transition:** The blue eye-piece flickers, dissolving into a warm golden hue. | **Music:** The dark drone abruptly halts. A single, resonant piano note strikes. <br> **SFX:** A 'glitch' sound, followed by the sound of a screen door creaking open. | "One soldier... is about to remember... where he really came from." |
| **0:25-0:40** | **Cinematic Shot:** 1950s Kodachrome style. A golden wheat field in rural Kansas. A quaint farmhouse. <br><br> **Mid Shot:** An elderly human couple (Nana and Pop-Pop) in overalls and an apron, lovingly holding a baby-sized Dalek casing wrapped in a knit blanket. | **Music:** Warm, nostalgic acoustic guitar fingerpicking begins. <br> **SFX:** Birds chirping, wind in the wheat, a cow mooing in the distance. | "He wasn't born in a factory. He was found... in a cornfield." |
| **0:40-0:55** | **Montage:** <br> 1. The small Dalek 'helping' Nana bake a pie (whisk taped to plunger). <br> 2. Pop-Pop teaching the Dalek to fish in a creek (fishing rod taped to laser arm). <br> 3. The Dalek sitting on a tractor. | **SFX:** Laughter of old people, splashing water, tractor engine chugging. | "Raised by Nana and Pop-Pop. They didn't see a killing machine. They saw... their little Rusty." |
| **0:55-1:10** | **Wide Shot:** A tiny red schoolhouse. <br><br> **Group Shot:** 5 children posing for a class photo. 4 human kids and one Dalek wearing a propeller beanie hat. <br><br> **Shot:** A montage of the town citizens: The Baker holding a baguette, The Teacher cleaning a chalkboard, The Cop tipping his hat. | **Music:** Swells into an uplifting, folk-orchestral melody. <br> **SFX:** School bell ringing, children shouting "Rusty's here!" | "In a town with one baker... one teacher... one cop... and a graduating class of five... he was just one of the guys." |
| **1:10-1:25** | **Action Shot:** The Dalek playing hide and seek, poorly hiding behind a thin tree. <br><br> **Shot:** The Dalek at prom, a corsage taped to his dome, sitting next to a punch bowl. | **SFX:** A bicycle bell. The sound of a Dalek gliding over gravel. | "They taught him to read. They taught him to fish. And they taught him the most dangerous weapon of all..." |
| **1:25-1:40** | **Dramatic Shift:** Back to the bleak alien landscape. The Dalek is surrounded by other angry Daleks. <br><br> **Close Up:** The Dalek's eye-stalk quivers with emotion. | **Music:** Music stops. Dead silence. <br> **SFX:** Weapons powering up. | "...Love." |
| **1:40-1:50** | **Key Scene:** The Dalek faces the Supreme Dalek. <br><br> **Visual:** The Dalek extends his plunger, holding a warm apple pie. | **SFX:** High pitched mechanical whine. <br> **Dalek Voice (High pitched, modulated):** "WOULD... YOU... LIKE... SOME... PIE?" | **(VO returns)**<br>"He's going home. And he's bringing dessert." |
| **1:50-1:55** | **Rapid Montage:** The Dalek hugging the cop. The Dalek gliding fast down a country road with the 4 friends riding on his casing. Explosion of confetti. | **Music:** Triumphant, soaring finale. | "This summer..." |
| **1:55-1:58** | **Title Card:** A DALEK COMES HOME. <br> *(Subtitle: EXTERMINATE THE LONELINESS)* | **SFX:** A single hawk screech. | "A Dalek Comes Home." |
| **1:58-2:00** | **Post-Title Scene:** The Dalek trying to blow out birthday candles on a cake, but accidentally shoots a laser beam, exploding the cake. | **SFX:** Laser blast. *Poof*. <br> **Dalek Voice:** "UH... OH." | (Silence) |
