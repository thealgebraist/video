# Movie Trailer Production Script: "The Banana Quest"
**Duration:** 30 Seconds
**Genre:** Adventure / Family
**Voiceover Style:** Enthusiastic, cinematic narrator.
**Music Bed:** Upbeat, whimsical orchestral adventure theme.

---

| Scene | Visual Description | Audio / VO |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **01** | **Close up:** A chimp (Charlie) wearing a tiny explorer's hat, looking intensely at a map showing a "Golden Banana". | **VO:** "One chimp..." |
| **02** | **Wide shot:** Charlie packing a small vintage suitcase with a toothbrush and a magnifying glass. | **VO:** "...one dream..." |
| **03** | **Medium shot:** Charlie standing on a platform as a massive, steam-puffing train pulls into the station. | **SFX:** Loud steam whistle. **VO:** "...and a ticket to the ultimate prize." |
| **04** | **Interior shot:** Charlie sitting in a plush velvet train seat, looking out at passing mountains. | **Music:** Whimsical strings swell. |
| **05** | **Close up:** Charlie sharing his seat with a confused penguin in a tuxedo. | **SFX:** Train chugging rhythm. |
| **06** | **Action shot:** The train crossing a precarious bridge over a jungle. | **VO:** "Across the Great Divide..." |
| **07** | **Wide shot:** Charlie stepping off the train in a bustling, futuristic city made of fruit shapes. | **VO:** "...to the city of legends." |
| **08** | **Close up:** A golden, glowing banana resting on a velvet cushion in a shop window. | **SFX:** Angelic choir note. |
| **09** | **Action shot:** Charlie sprinting through the city streets towards the shop. | **VO:** "He's not just hungry..." |
| **10** | **Close up:** Charlie's hand reaches for the banana. | **VO:** "...he's on a mission." |
| **11** | **Title Card:** THE BANANA QUEST. Coming this Summer. | **Music:** Big orchestral finish. |
| **12** | **Post-Title:** Charlie tries to peel the banana, but it's too slippery and flies out of his hands. | **SFX:** *Slide whistle*. Charlie makes a confused chimp sound. |
